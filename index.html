Cake Tips and Techniques

Professional Tips

When baking a cheesecake it is important not to overbake it. You only want to bake it until the middle has a slight wiggle.

It is important that you use a water bath, discussed at the right, to ensure the easy baking of your cheesecake.

To create a water bath, use a pan that will allow you to fill it with boiling water that goes halfway up the spring pan in which the cake was placed.
